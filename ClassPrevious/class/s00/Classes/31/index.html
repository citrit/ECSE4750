ECSE-4750 Computer Graphics, Fall 1999
Class 31, Thurs Nov 11

Homogeneous Coords, ctd

We reviewed and extended homogeneous coordinates for rotation etc, including for the infinite point at the end of a line, and the intersection of two parallel lines. We haven't done them for projections yet.

Clipping

We saw the Sutherland-Hodgman algorithm for clipping a polygon, and the viewing pipeline that got SGI started. Clipping is described in the text in great detail.
